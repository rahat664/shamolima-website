About Us

SHAMOLIMA has been involved in providing Project Support Services in Bangladesh ever since the inception of the company in 1983. SHAMOLIMA has specialized in this sector through our extensive experience of providing Professional Consultancy Services, Supplying Project Materials, Providing Construction and Erection facilities, Supplying Oil and Gas field related equipment, Power Plant Heavy Engine, Transformer & other heavy material transportation from Mother Vessel to project site after Shipping & Customs clearance under our own license. Telecommunications equipment and services, and Catering & Housekeeping Services, Warehousing, Crew Handling, Crew Sign ON/OFF at different port, certified man power service & Meet&Greet service arrival/departure personnel at every international/domestic terminal in side the country.
